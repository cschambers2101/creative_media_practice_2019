# BTEC Creative Media Practice 2019

## Overview

Welcome to your new specification for the Pearson BTEC Level 3 Nationals in Creative Media Practice. This qualification brings you the best of the BTEC tradition of developing independent, vocationally aware learners through the right balance between teaching/learning and assessment. The qualification has been designed around holistic, explorative and integrated creative teaching approaches that are conducive to deep learning and skills development.

The qualifications support a curriculum where learners are able to continually revisit their approaches to the making and understanding of media practice, adding levels of refinement, sophistication and precision to their practice as they progress.
